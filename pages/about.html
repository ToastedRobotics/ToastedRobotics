About Us  

2405T (3388A)

221W (Alliance Partner)

Toasted Robotics (2405T) is a high performance, Calgary-based VRC team. Our team consists of six 10th grade students from Sir Winston Churchill High School. Formed in 2019 under the team number 3388A, Our team was based on the need for higher performance and greater opportunities, which were not readily achievable at our previous organization. This year, we plan on ranking top 3 in Alberta and qualifying for the World Championships. In addition to this, we are aiming to place in the top 100 worldwide in combined skills. We plan on attending tournaments in Alberta and BC. Overall, we plan on becoming a VRC oriented organization based in Alberta which will help to mentor and provide resources for younger students interested in STEM and robotics.

Mission

We provide young Canadians with opportunities to excel in the STEM field through the VEX platform.

Vision

To be a significant organization in the VEX community which provides exemplary robotics education to the Greater Calgary region, and empowers Canadians of all backgrounds.

Our Values

  COMMUNITY  

We strive to give back and our community (Calgary and surrounding area) and to pass knowledge forward. This is usually in the form of volunteering, tips and suggestions both in-person and online (Discord and Instagram), the publishing of mechanisms, and through our involvement in the creation of strategies and designs in the VEX community.

  EXCELLENCE  

We set high expectations and hold all our teammates to the highest standard. We apply our diverse skill sets to the best of our ability and aim to both out-work and out-perform the competition.

  RESPECT  

The VEX community is built on trust, acceptance, tolerance, and respect. Our team prides itself on our respect and acknowledgement for both competitors, teammates, suppliers, and mentors. We emphasize the positive representation of our community, sponsors, and organization at all events we attend.

  TEAMWORK  

Building good relationships with our team members is very important to us. We focus on team building and trust by bringing together like-minded, driven, and motivated individuals. Being friends on and off the field is essential to our team.

  WORK ETHIC  

Knowledge is nothing without motivation, and our team prides ourselves on our determination, work ethic, and motivation. We inspire others with our focus and drive, as well as help motivate our teammates, peers, and community.
